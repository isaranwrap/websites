What is the CASHE Substrate?
---

**CASHE** stands for:
- Chinese
- Arabic
- Spanish
- Hindi
- English

It is a form of cultural capital. There are over 1 billion people speaking Chinese/Hindi/English as their first or second language and 500 million people speaking Arabic or Spanish as their primary or secondary language; the necessity for establishing nets to catch and enhance language-learning goes without saying. Students of these languages have a special place in my heart and the substrate is an effort to create a scaffholding for optimal learning of core key cultural elements in each of these languages. Therefore, the *CASHE Substrate* is a soil for growing connections in the CASHE languages.
